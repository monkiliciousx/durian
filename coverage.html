What Is Covered

Accidental screen crack protection

A valid claim may result in the screen being repaired, or a replacement solution determined by the service provider according to the final approved terms.

Covered

  • A screen crack caused by an unforeseen accidental event.
  • One repair or replacement service during the coverage period.
  • New or refurbished parts that are equivalent to new in performance and reliability.
  • A replacement of like kind and quality where repair is not economical or parts are obsolete, subject to provider discretion.

Important conditions

  • The damage must not be deliberate, reckless, fraudulent or caused by misuse.
  • The claim must be supported with the required documents and damage evidence.
  • Unauthorised repair or modification can terminate eligibility.
  • The plan is non-transferable and non-refundable under the supplied terms.
  • Faulty parts or a replaced device may become the service provider’s property.
Important Exclusions

What is not covered?

The full contract contains a long exclusion list. The categories below make the main exclusions easier to understand.

Mechanical, electrical and component problems

Mechanical or electrical breakdown, battery packs, speakers, charging accessories, cables, buttons, display-panel wear, voltage damage, short circuits and similar component failures are excluded under the supplied contract.

Liquid, fire, theft and environmental damage

Water or flood damage, fire, theft, burglary, lightning, storms, corrosion, animal or insect infestation and other listed external events are not covered.

Wear, appearance and accessories

Wear and tear, rust, mould, discolouration, cosmetic damage, the housing, frame, decorative parts, chargers, earphones and other accessories are excluded.

Misuse, negligence and unauthorised repair

Deliberate acts, recklessness, negligence, misuse, abuse, improper installation, wrong voltage, unauthorised repair or modification and failure to follow the manufacturer’s instructions are excluded.

Manufacturer defects, recalls and software

Manufacturer defects, product recalls, computer viruses, software errors, installed programs, setup assistance and data-related issues are not covered.

Invalid or unsupported claims

Claims may be rejected where the phone was registered late, the IMEI or documents were altered, the damage cannot be proven, fraud is involved, or required information is not supplied.

Very important: the current supplied contract does not support the homepage claim that liquid damage, battery issues, speaker failure or general phone malfunction are covered. Those statements should only return if the supplier provides updated written terms confirming broader protection.

Your responsibilities

  • Provide accurate and complete information.
  • Respond to requests for phone, software and incident details.
  • Follow packing, pickup and service instructions.
  • Do not arrange unauthorised repairs or modifications.
  • Inform the provider if personal information changes.

Back up your phone before service

Repair or replacement may result in the loss of programs, photos, files or other phone data. The supplied terms limit responsibility for data loss and loss of use while the phone is being serviced. Customers should back up and remove sensitive information before handing over the device.

Plan Termination

When does the plan end?

  • When the plan reaches the end of its coverage period.
  • After the covered phone has been repaired or replaced.
  • If the phone is sold, disposed of, lost or repossessed.
  • If the phone is repaired or modified by an unauthorised party.
  • If fraud, misrepresentation or a breach of the plan terms is discovered.
