FAQ

Questions? We have answers.

This demo includes the main questions a potential customer would normally ask.

What information is needed to register my phone?

You may need your personal information, phone details, device identifier, purchase date and proof of purchase.

Can I submit a claim online?

Yes. The future claim portal can allow customers to submit details, photos and supporting documents online.

Will pickup be arranged?

Pickup can be arranged after the claim has been reviewed and approved according to the final operating process.

Are the prices shown here final?

No. This first GitHub version is a visual prototype. Pricing, coverage and legal wording should be updated before launch.
