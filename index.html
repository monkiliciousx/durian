Common Phone Problems

Your phone does a lot. Until it doesn’t.

One drop, one splash or one weak battery can interrupt work, payments, calls, photos and almost everything else in your day.

Cracked Screen

One slip is all it takes. Cracks block your view, affect touch response and can spread quickly.

✦ Harder to use every day

No Sound

Calls, voice notes, videos and important alerts become difficult to hear or completely silent.

✦ Missed calls and alerts
⚡

Battery Issues

Your phone drains too quickly, switches off unexpectedly or refuses to charge when you need it.

✦ Always searching for a charger

Phone Not Responding

Frozen screen, endless loading or no touch response. Suddenly, nothing works when you need it most.

✦ Work and daily tasks stop

Camera Problem

Camera faults can ruin photos, videos, product shots and important memories when you need them most.

✦ Photos and videos are affected

FAQ

Questions? We have answers.

This demo includes the main questions a potential customer would normally ask.

What information is needed to register my phone?

You may need your personal information, phone details, device identifier, purchase date and proof of purchase.

Can I submit a claim online?

Yes. The future claim portal can allow customers to submit details, photos and supporting documents online.

Will pickup be arranged?

Pickup can be arranged after the claim has been reviewed and approved according to the final operating process.

How much does the protection cost?

The current one-time prices are RM78 for phones below RM3,000, RM178 for phones valued from RM3,000 to RM5,000, and RM278 for phones valued from RM5,000 to RM8,000. Final eligibility and policy terms still apply.
